# GPT Restore

> **Validate and restore eMMC GPT partition tables on Nintendo Switch consoles**

[![License: GPL v2](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-GPL%20v2-blue.svg)](https://www.gnu.org/licenses/old-licenses/gpl-2.0.en.html)
[![Platform](https://img.shields.io/badge/platform-Nintendo%20Switch-red.svg)](https://www.nintendo.com/switch/)

A payload designed to recover from corrupted or damaged GPT tables from modchip installation, or user error during partitioning or formatting.

The tool supports both 32GB and 64GB eMMC configurations across all retail Switch hardware revisions.

## USE AT YOUR OWN RISK

- This tool is intended for advanced users and performs low-level eMMC operations. Incorrect usage can lead to permanent brick of your device.
- Always create a full eMMC backup before using this tool, and ensure your device has sufficient battery charge or is connected to power. Do NOT power off during the restore process
